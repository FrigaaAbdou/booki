# Booki – Product Context

## Overview

Booki is a simple booking system designed for small and medium businesses that manage appointments.

It helps businesses move away from WhatsApp-based scheduling and manual appointment management.

Booki is not a SaaS marketplace.
It is a customizable booking system deployed individually for each client.

---

## Target Market

Initial Market: Algeria  
Future Expansion: Global

Primary Users:
- Barbers
- Beauty salons
- Clinics
- Doctors
- Gyms
- Coaches
- Car wash services
- Training centers
- Any appointment-based business

---

## Core Problem

Small businesses currently:

- Manage bookings manually
- Use WhatsApp for scheduling
- Miss calls and messages
- Experience double bookings
- Have no centralized calendar
- Lack professionalism online

---

## Solution

Booki provides:

- A booking website for the business
- An admin dashboard for managing appointments
- Mobile-friendly design
- Customizable branding per client

The system is installed and configured for each business.

---

## Value Proposition

Simple. Practical. Affordable.

Main Promise:
"Let your clients book you online."

Booki modernizes local businesses without complexity.

---

## Product Features

### For Customers (End Clients)

- Online booking 24/7
- Service list display
- Time slot selection
- Booking confirmation
- Business contact and location

### For Business Owners

- Calendar view (daily / weekly)
- Appointment management
- Client list & history
- Staff management (optional)
- Booking status control

---

## Business Model

Booki is not a subscription SaaS.

It is:

- A customizable product
- Installed per client
- Adaptable branding
- Flexible pricing depending on needs

Possible models:
- One-time setup fee
- Optional maintenance package
- Custom feature pricing
